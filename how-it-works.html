Handling of Projects at SleeperDev

See how we maintain consistency in freelance work.

Ordering a remote project has never been such a streamlined process.

The process of ordering a project requires only 3 simple steps

Describe your requirements

Tell us the main aim of your project along and we can discuss the design and framework details along with it. After that, our developers estimate the time required for the project and present you with the price quote.

We get to work.

Once you accept the quote, we assemble a team of finest developers, designers and project managers. You receive the contact information of the entire team so you can inform the team about any change requirements in the future.

You receive your project.

We deliver you a working video of the project and if you're satisfied with our work, you send in the payment and receive the completed project. You get to keep the contact information of all the people assigned to the project for future references.

THAT'S IT!

Freelance work completed in 3 easy steps!
