Why work for SleeperDev?

Because why not?

Working with SleeperDev is easy and commitment free!

We value your time and hardwork at SleeperDev and hence, we make it our priority that all the employees are satisfied with the work environment we provide. We aim to make freelance work easier for both employees and employers. Here are some of the reasons why you will love working for SleeperDev.

Remote

Many students and freelancers cannot have a full time job and hence prefer remote work. Almost all the work assigned to you will be remote. Thus, making it easier for college students to gain some industrial experience while finishing your degree. SleeperDev also benefits full time employees that like to utilize their weekends with some freelance work.

No Commitments!

We aim to have a big roster of available employees so whenever a project comes up, it can be assigned to people who are available to work at that particular time. Thus, keeping a simple formula of working only when available so that you don't have to skip any classes.

Absolutely Free for you!

We don't charge any upfront costs from our employees. From the interview to the finish of the project, you will be the one getting paid for your skills.

Each of our employee gets paid for the work they do and hence we do not follow a traditional salary system. You get paid for your hard work, no more, no less.

Gain industry level experience.

We believe that one learns the most through practicing and experience. With SleeperDev you would be handling real industry projects. Therefore, working with SleeperDev will not only become a source of income for you but will also prepare you for the job sector for future.

So what are you waiting for?

Book an interview today and kick start you career with SleeperDev. Currently hiring in all fields. We are eager to meet you!
